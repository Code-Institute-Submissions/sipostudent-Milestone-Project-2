GAME OBJECTIVE
Replicate the pattern showed by Simon. With each round, a new step is added to the pattern making the game much more difficult.
HOW TO WIN
Repeat 10 patterns correctly and SIMON will salute you with a congratulations message in acknowledgement of your triumph.
PLAY MODES & CONTROLS
'Strict Mode' restarts the game whenever an incorrect field is chosen. 'Normal Mode' only repeats the pattern subsequent to the wrong button being pressed.
Control the game by pressing the relevant alpha character on a keyboard or by directly clicking buttons with a mouse cursor.
